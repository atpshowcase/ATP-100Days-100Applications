## First Steps

1. **Open your browser** and go to http://localhost:3000

2. **Write your first entry**:
   - You'll see today's date automatically selected
   - Think about the most storyworthy moment of your day
   - Write one sentence (up to 280 characters)
   - Click "Save Entry"

3. **Explore the features**:
   - Use the arrow buttons to navigate to different dates
   - Click "History" to see all your entries
   - Click "Export as Text" to download your journal

## Daily Practice

**Make it a habit:**
- Set a reminder for the same time each day (evening works best)
- Reflect on your day before writing
- Ask yourself: "What moment today would make a good story?"
- Keep it to one sentence - be specific and concise

## Example Entries

Good storyworthy sentences:
- "My daughter said 'I love you' unprompted for the first time today."
- "I finally stood up for myself in the meeting and everyone listened."
- "The stranger at the coffee shop paid for my order and smiled without saying a word."

## Tips

- **Don't overthink it** - Your first thought is often the best
- **Be specific** - Include details that make the moment unique
- **Focus on change** - Moments where something shifted
- **Capture emotion** - What made you feel something?

## Technical Notes

- All entries are saved in your browser's localStorage
- No data leaves your computer
- Works offline once loaded
- Use the same browser to access your entries

When you're done:
1. Go to your terminal
2. Press `Ctrl + C` to stop the development server

## Restarting Later

To use the app again:
1. Open terminal in the project folder
2. Run: `npm run dev`
3. Open http://localhost:3000
